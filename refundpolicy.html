Refund Policy

Clear terms for our digital services.

1. Digital Service Nature

Due to the nature of digital marketing services and the immediate allocation of resources/ad-spend upon order placement, we maintain a strict refund policy. Please read the following terms carefully before selecting a plan.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Before Campaign Start: If you request a cancellation before we have initiated your campaign or allocated ad-spend, you are eligible for a 90% refund (10% processing fee applies).
  • After Campaign Start: Once a campaign has been launched on YouTube or Google Ads, we cannot offer a full refund as the funds have been committed to the advertising platforms.

3. Eligibility for Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be considered at our discretion if:

  • The service provided deviates significantly from the selected plan's description.
  • Technical errors on our part prevent the campaign from reaching its minimum milestones.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Consultation fees, setup fees, and funds already spent on advertising platforms (Google/YouTube) are strictly non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund?

To initiate a request, please email billing@adspro.example with your Order ID and reason for the request. We aim to process and respond to all inquiries within 5-7 business days.
